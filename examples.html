Grootstrap examples

What the hell?

Did you know that Bootstrap features approximately 0% Vin Diesel? That's just not acceptable in this day and age, is it? So I've picked a cultural icon from a Marvel movie and made it into a thing you could theoretically actually use in production. For those of us who want to cover our markup in excessive, hard to read references to Guardians of the Galaxy, this is the grid framework for you. Grab it from Github if you want.

Rows

Make a row with "we-are-groot", any capitalisation allowed, hyphens optional.

Columns

The number of o's in the word groot dictates how many columns to span - i-am-groot will span 1, i-am-grooot will span 2, and so on.

Responsive

Grootstrap is mobile first and comes with two baked in breakpoints - Use titlecase to indicate a tablet column width, and uppercase to indicate a desktop column width.

So, a <div class="i-am-grooooooooooooot I-Am-Grooooooot I-AM-GROOOOOT">l</div> will span 12 columns on mobile, 6 on tablet and 4 on desktop.
